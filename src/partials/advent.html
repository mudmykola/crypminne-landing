Advantages of cloud mining

The pros of cloud mining include the following points:

  • No equipment purchase costs. Your costs are related only to the rental of facilities.

  • No costs associated with maintenance and repair. All of this falls on the shoulders of the company that provides cloud mining services. You don't have to deal with all these issues yourself or hire specialists.

  • No need to upgrade your equipment yourself. Cloud mining services do it themselves and are interested in timely upgrades.

  • No electricity costs. The equipment is located in the company's data centers. All costs are already included in the service fees.

  • You will not have to deal with organizational issues, rent space for the farm.

  • An opportunity to start with a small capital investment. The size of the minimum contract is from 1,000 Russian roubles.
